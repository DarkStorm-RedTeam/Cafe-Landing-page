Our Story

Crafted withPassion

Born from a deep love for coffee culture, Noir & Brew opened its doors in Alexandria in 2016. We source our beans directly from single-origin farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Yemen — partnering with farmers who share our obsession for quality.

Our baristas aren't just coffee makers — they're storytellers. Each cup is calibrated with precision, served with warmth, and designed to transport you somewhere extraordinary.
